Project overview

Techward Infosolutions designed and developed the AFLINX App, a comprehensive healthcare scheduling and workforce engagement platform used by personnel at the 177th USAF Air Force Base. The app enables users to schedule medical and dental appointments, respond to alerts, participate in polls, and provide feedback—all from a unified digital interface. In addition to healthcare coordination, AFLINX includes powerful career optimization tools that allow users to list work experiences, upload training certificates, and better manage their professional profiles.

Built with Xamarin for Android and iOS, along with a complementary responsive Web App, the solution delivers a seamless cross-platform experience. Our team focused on performance, user-centric design, and secure data handling to meet the rigorous operational and compliance needs of a military healthcare environment.

Client challenges & requirements

The USAF 177th base required a secure, intuitive, and reliable mobile platform to improve access to medical and dental scheduling while offering enhanced communication tools for alerts, polls, and feedback. Legacy workflows were paper-based or fragmented across systems, leading to inefficiencies and a lack of real-time visibility for personnel and administrators alike.

Key challenges included ensuring data privacy, real-time messaging, seamless scheduling processes, and easy navigation for users with varying technical comfort levels. The platform also needed a robust career module that supports training documentation, experience tracking, and professional growth tools—all backed by secure, enterprise-grade backend services.

  • Seamless appointment scheduling for medical and dental services
  • Real-time alert, poll, and feedback messaging
  • Career optimization features with document upload capabilities
  • Cross-platform support (Android, iOS, Web)
  • Secure authentication and data privacy compliant with military standards

Project solution

Techward Infosolutions delivered a fully integrated, multi-platform solution built with Xamarin, ensuring near-native performance across Android and iOS devices alongside a responsive web application. We created a modern, easy-to-navigate user experience that simplifies appointment bookings, improves communication, and empowers users to manage their professional profiles.

The scheduling module allows users to view available time slots, book appointments, and receive confirmations and reminders. The alert and messaging system provides instant communication between administrators and personnel. The career optimization tools enable users to track work history, upload certificates, and maintain an up-to-date professional portfolio—supporting both career development and deployment readiness.

To ensure reliability and privacy, the backend was developed with strong data encryption, secure authentication, and scalable infrastructure designed to handle concurrent use by base personnel.

Impact & Results

  • Streamlined medical & dental appointment management
  • Real-time alerts, polls, and feedback engagement
  • Enhanced user empowerment through career tools
  • Secure, cross-platform accessibility across mobile and web
  • Scalable and compliant solution for military healthcare workflows
